About Us

TeacherDirectory is a Pakistani portal, where you can find most a basic profile overview of most University teachers all over Pakistan. Information including courses taught, a basic description, profile pictures, everything is available on this website. This was originally the first semester project of CS101 at DHA Suffa University, developed by Kumail & Shayan.
